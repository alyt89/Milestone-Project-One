Discover Ski Cross

Our Aim

We want to spread the word about Ski Cross and give people of all ages all across the UK the chance to experience the thrill of this unique sport.

Getting into a new sport can feel quite daunting sometimes so we are focused on not only giving people of all abilities the chance to practice Ski Cross but also providing the basic equipment needed at our events.

We are passionate about getting people involved in the sport through various dry Ski Slope facilities across the country, who knows, the 2026 Winter Olympics could well be a Ski Cross Club member!

Our History

We were set up in 2018 after the success of Team GB in the Winter Olympics sparked a huge interest in winter sports. We held our first event in London the same year where almost 100 people attended.

Since then we have held 30 events allowing people to discover this amazing sport reaching out to around 3,000 people at 5 different locations across the country.

What is Ski Cross?

Ski Cross is a sport that mixes different disciplines and skills you may already know from traditional skiing. The main difference with Ski cross and the majority of other disciplines is instead of being time-trialled or skiers going down individually, 4 skiers compete at the same time to get to the finishing line first.
