Our Aim

We want to spread the word about Ski Cross and give people of all ages all across the UK the chance to experience the thrill of the sport.

Skiing can be an expensive sport and we want everyone to feel included so we have a range of equipment to offer to those who attend our events.

Our History

Established in 2018 after the success of Team GB in the Winter Olympics we held our first event in London.

Since then we have held 30 events allowing people to discover this amazing sport reaching out to around 3,000 people at 5 different locations across the country.
